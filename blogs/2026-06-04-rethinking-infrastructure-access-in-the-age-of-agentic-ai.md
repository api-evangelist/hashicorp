Traditional identity and access management approaches fall short for autonomous AI systems that can reason, invoke tools, and access services across infrastructure in unpredictable ways. HashiCorp Boundary provides solutions including unique per-agent identities, just-in-time credential provisioning via Vault integration, session-focused controls, and comprehensive audit logging to secure agentic AI workflows at scale.
